# Privacy Policy — My Stack by Myopedia.org Chrome Extension

**Last updated: May 13, 2026**
**Published by: Container SIA**

---

## Overview

My Stack by Myopedia.org is a Chrome browser extension that allows users to save articles from myopedia.org to a personal reading list. This policy explains clearly and completely what data the extension handles.

---

## Data We Collect

This extension collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

- The URL, title, and subtitle of articles you choose to save
- - The date and time you saved each article
 
  - No other data is collected. No personal information, no browsing history outside of myopedia.org, no device identifiers, no IP address, no usage analytics.
 
  - ---

  ## Where Your Data Is Stored

  All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) on your own device. Your saved articles never leave your device. They are not transmitted to any server, database, third party, or to us.

  ---

  ## Data We Do Not Collect

  To be explicit, this extension does not:

  - Collect, transmit, or share any personal information
  - - Track your browsing activity on any website
    - - Send any data to external servers or third parties
      - - Use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers
        - - Access any data outside of myopedia.org pages
          - - Collect data from users under the age of 18
           
            - ---

            ## Third Parties

            This extension does not integrate with, share data with, or rely on any third-party services. No data is sold, rented, or shared with any party under any circumstances.

            ---

            ## Permissions

            The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

            - `storage` — to save your article list locally on your device
            - - `sidePanel` — to display your saved list in the Chrome side panel
              - - `host_permissions: myopedia.org` — to inject the save button on Myopedia article pages only
               
                - No broader host permissions are requested or used.
               
                - ---

                ## Data Deletion

                You can delete all saved data at any time by clicking the ✕ button on individual articles in the side panel, or by removing the extension from Chrome entirely. Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all locally stored data.

                ---

                ## Changes to This Policy

                If this policy is updated, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
